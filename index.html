Welcome

QuadText is a lightweight LaTeX editor written purely in Lua language.

Features

  • Written purely in Lua, small and portable.
  • Available for major operating systems.
  • Auto completion for LaTeX commands.
  • Syntax highlighting with several themes.
  • Extendable with built-in or your plugins.
  • Easily customizable with configuration files.

Download

You could download the latest QuadText releases from here.

At present, Linux and Mac OS X releases are unstable. Contributions are welcome to make QuadText a better LaTeX editor.
